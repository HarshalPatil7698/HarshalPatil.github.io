About Me

Hey there! I'm Harshal Patil, a passionate Data Analyst/BI Analyst with over a year of experience working in a leading MNC. While I started with a mechanical engineering degree, my journey led me to discover my true passion for diving deep into data.

I've honed some essential skills along the way, mastering tools like Excel, SQL, and Power BI. These skills empower me to dissect data effectively and extract meaningful insights.
